Your uploaded AI will play 100000 matches each versus three different agents; Random, Greedy, DeepQ. The total score is the sum of all wins minus the sum of all losses. The Random agent always plays a random move. The Greedy Agent plays the move that gives the highest score from the visible cards. The DeepQ agent uses a pretrained Neural Network to find the best move. The goal is to get the highest total score possible. Since these agents are pretty tough, it is quite possible to get a negative total score, even with a good model.
